Kottayam

The district is bordered by hills in the east, and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west. The area's geographic features include paddy fields, highlands, and hills. The name Kottayam is a combination of the words "kotta" and "akam" in the local language of Malayalam, meaning "interior of a fort". Kottayam has been involved in a number of political movements, including the 'Malayali Memorial' movement, Vaikom Satyagraha etc. Kottayam has a network of rivers, backwaters, ancient religious places, and hill stations.
